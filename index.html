About DevMentor

Built by Learners, For Learners

DevMentor was born from personal experience - the frustration of learning to code alone, late at night, with no one to ask for help. We realized that the best learning happens through conversation and guided discovery, not passive consumption.

By combining multi-agent AI architecture with proven teaching methodologies like the Socratic method, we've created something unique: an AI that doesn't just know the answer, but knows how to help YOU figure it out.
